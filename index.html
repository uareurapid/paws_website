Features

Everything your pet's health needs

From routine vaccines to weight milestones, PetPulse has a log entry for every moment of your pet's life.

💊

Health Log Library

Log vaccines, medications, vet visits, treatments, flea prevention, deworming, grooming, weight checks, and free-text notes — all timestamped and searchable.

Vaccine Medication Treatment Vet Visit Flea Deworming Grooming Weight Note
🔔

Smart Reminders

Set one-time or recurring reminders so you never miss a booster, monthly flea treatment, or annual vet visit.

📈

Weight Tracking

Log your pet's weight over time and see a visual chart to keep an eye on healthy growth and spot trends early.

📸

Photo Attachments

Attach up to 4 photos to any log entry — great for keeping before/after grooming shots or vet receipts.

🐾

Multi-pet Support

Manage a whole household of animals from one home screen. Dogs, cats, birds, rabbits, fish, turtles, hamsters… and more.

📋

Timeline View

A chronological feed of all health events across all your pets, so you always have the full picture at a glance.

How it works

Set up in under two minutes

No account, no subscription, no friction. Just open the app and start logging.

1

Add your pet

Enter their name, species, breed, and birthday. Snap a photo or skip it for now — you can always add one later.

2

Log health events

Choose a log type, add a title and optional notes, pick a date, and save. It takes seconds per entry.

3

Set reminders

From any log entry or pet profile, schedule a reminder. Get notified when it's due — right in your lock screen.

4

Stay on top of it all

Check the Reminders tab for what's coming up, and the Timeline for a full history across every pet.
